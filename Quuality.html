Cupful of purity

We really go a long way to give you the purest and the tastiest chai you’ve ever savored. Every cup of Chai Kings is rich with natural ingredients sourced from the finest tea plantations and from some of the highly reputed tea companies in Assam, Darjeeling and Nilgiri.

We have an obsession to offer you the purest chai, and hence we are ultra-conscious to ensure that no artificial elements are included in our preparation. We follow a systematic process which guarantees that there is consistency in quality at any given point of time. No compromises. No mediocrity. Just the pure joy of chai in every single cup.

Professionally driven

Frankly, you’ll love our team as much as you love our chai. Every single member at Chai Kings is trained to achieve perfection in sustaining high standards of quality and service. When it comes to creating a dependable workforce we are in a league of our own, since we offer thoughtfully created technology-based modules for training our staff.
